WHAT WE DO


GIS HUB aims to help new and aspiring professionals find their way into GIS technology and scripting programming languages such as Python and R through tutorial-based learning.

Dive into some of the basic concepts and skills needed to tackle real world problems utilizing geographical information systems and programming softwares. From learning about Arcpy 101 or getting your feet wet with some data science, GIS HUB is a great place to learn some awesome skills to succeed in your upcoming project!

WHO WE ARE


GIS HUB is created by people who are passionate about sharing knowledge and skills about geographical information systems in hopes that it will spark creativity and drive to conquer the issues our Earth faces today and foster the leaders of tomorrow.

Feel free to visit our Introduction page to find out more or to contact us!
